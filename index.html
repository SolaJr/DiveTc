What is Dive Chat?

Group messaging, planning events, and keeping up with your friend groups shouldn't suck, but in our 4 years at a 50k+ person campus, it did. The platforms we used just didn't cut it, and there was nothing better we could turn to. That's why we set out to build Dive.

Easy Group Messaging

Dive uses channels - small, topic-specific chats within larger groups - to keep communication organized and purpose-driven. Users can easily turn notifications on or off for each channel, only receiving alerts for the content they care about without missing out on the important things.

Event Planning

Dive is optimized for planning both real-world and virtual events. Our Let's Hang feature takes the hassle out of coordinating hangouts, and the social calendar let's users see their upcoming events at a glance. Built-in admin privileges make managing events and groups simple and seamless.

Experience Making

In addition to your organization, local events around you can be found and used for your members in their personal time!

Fun With Friends

Converse with your friend groups and create plans effortlessly without dozens of back-and-forth messages. Pick up Dive in minutes with our 20-second onboarding process and never look back.

Greek Life

Keep your social organizations on the same page with Dive's integrated social calendar, high-priority notifications visible to all members, and the ability to delete messages as needed.
